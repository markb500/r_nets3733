Overview

This app is intended for use as a supplemental revision aid in your preparation for the Electrical Principles exam. It should be used alongside the practice questions provided in the training notes and in consultation with your Tutor.

Instructions For Use

Choose a button from the left column for the subject you wish to revise. Each click will create a new sum. Once you have completed your answer on paper, click the ‘solution’ button to see a full, worked solution. A counter within the Solution button shows the number of times the solution has been viewed. Note that, where calculations use the results from previous calculations, the rounded figure is used.

Link To Training Notes

Under each question, a link is provided which will take you to the relevant area of the training notes.
